Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

TinyRest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we process the following data:

  • Account Data: Email address and User ID (via Google Firebase Authentication).
  • Child Data: Name (optional), Date of Birth (required for age-appropriate sleep calculations), and Gender.
  • Usage Data: Sleep logs (start/end times), partner notes, and app interaction history.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the TinyRest service:

  • Synchronization: To sync data in real-time between parents/caregivers.
  • AI Analysis: We use Google Gemini AI to generate sleep predictions. Note: When requesting predictions, we send only the baby's age (in months) and anonymized sleep logs. We do NOT share your baby's name or your email address with the AI model.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption in transit and secure database rules to ensure only authorized family members can access your baby's data.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Export your data.

5. Account & Data Deletion

In-App Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data instantly inside the app by navigating to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account.

Web Request: If you have uninstalled the app and wish to delete your account, please email us at reviewer@tinyrest.ie with the subject "DELETE ACCOUNT". We will verify your identity and delete your data within 30 days.
